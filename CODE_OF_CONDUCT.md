# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Sentra Terminal is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free environment for all contributors, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are dedicated to:
- Creating a safe and respectful community
- Valuing the contributions of every individual
- Fostering an environment of mutual respect and learning
- Addressing concerns promptly and fairly

---

## Expected Behavior

Community members are expected to:

### Be Respectful
- Treat all members with dignity and respect
- Use inclusive language and avoid offensive terms
- Listen actively and value different perspectives
- Acknowledge and respect others' boundaries

### Be Collaborative
- Support and help other community members
- Share knowledge generously
- Give and receive constructive feedback gracefully
- Assume good intentions

### Be Professional
- Keep discussions on-topic and constructive
- Avoid personal attacks or disparaging comments
- Represent the project positively in public spaces
- Respect intellectual property and citations

### Be Inclusive
- Welcome people of all backgrounds and experience levels
- Help newcomers get started
- Adapt communication styles for accessibility
- Challenge biased or exclusionary comments

---

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are unacceptable and will not be tolerated:

### Harassment
- Unwelcome sexual advances, requests for sexual favors, or sexual comments
- Discriminatory jokes or comments about protected characteristics
- Deliberate intimidation, stalking, or following
- Harmful, abusive, or threatening language

### Discrimination
- Exclusion or discrimination based on any protected characteristic
- Stereotyping or making assumptions about individuals
- Systemic exclusion from community activities
- Weaponizing identity characteristics in arguments

### Abuse of Process
- Repeatedly reporting non-violating content as violations
- Using reports to silence or intimidate others
- Posting the same message repeatedly (spam)
- Spam, phishing, or malware distribution

### Other Misconduct
- Sharing others' private information without consent
- Impersonating someone else
- Violating GitHub's Terms of Service
- Conduct that would be illegal in most jurisdictions

---

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All project repositories and discussions
- Project social media accounts
- Project events (online and in-person)
- One-on-one communications related to project work
- Communications anywhere if they affect the project community

The Code of Conduct applies whether a violation occurs in project spaces or outside project spaces if the behavior affects the safety and well-being of project members.

---

## Enforcement

### Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior:

1. **Email**: conduct@pacifica.fi (confidential)
2. **GitHub Report**: Use GitHub's abuse reporting feature
3. **Direct Message**: Message a maintainer (only if not about maintainer)

**Include:**
- Your contact information
- Incident description and dates
- Parties involved
- Screenshots or links
- Any context/history

### Investigation

We will:
1. Acknowledge receipt within 24 hours
2. Investigate promptly and confidentially
3. Interview involved parties separately
4. Determine appropriate action
5. Notify reporter of outcome (if safe)

### Consequences

Violations may result in:
1. **First Offense**: Warning and discussion
2. **Second Offense**: Temporary suspension (1-30 days)
3. **Serious Offense**: Permanent ban
4. **Illegal Activity**: Report to authorities

Severity and context determine the response. We reserve the right to:
- Remove comments or posts
- Suspend or ban accounts
- Report to GitHub
- Report to law enforcement if necessary

### Appeals

- Banned members may appeal after 90 days
- Send appeal to conduct@pacifica.fi
- Committee will review and respond

## Community Guidelines by Space

### GitHub Issues & Discussions
- Stay on topic
- Use search before posting duplicates
- Follow the issue template
- Be clear and provide context
- Respect others' time in reviews

### Pull Request Reviews
- Review code, not the person
- Ask questions, don't make demands
- Acknowledge good work and effort
- Suggest improvements respectfully
- Accept feedback gracefully

### Discord/Chat (if exists)
- Use appropriate channels for topics
- Avoid excessive notifications
- No harassment or spam
- Keep images/links relevant
- Respect quiet hours

### In-Person Events
- Respect personal boundaries
- No photography without consent
- Include everyone in conversations
- Report issues to organizers immediately
- Follow venue and event policies

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Maintainers commit to:
- Enforcing the Code of Conduct fairly and consistently
- Explaining why actions are taken
- Maintaining confidentiality during investigations
- Recusing themselves if directly involved in a report
- Seeking guidance from the community when needed
- Being transparent about enforcement decisions (appropriately)
